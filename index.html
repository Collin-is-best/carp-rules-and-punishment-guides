Introduction

Welcome to the CARP RP Server for Roblox ERLC. This guide contains all the necessary rules and guidelines to ensure an enjoyable and fair roleplaying experience.

RP Rules

All members must adhere to these RP rules:

  • Maintain in-character behavior at all times during RP sessions.
  • Respect other players and their roles.
  • No metagaming or powergaming.
  • Use appropriate language and behavior that fits the RP setting.
  • Report any rule violations to moderators immediately.

Punishment Guidelines

Below are the punishment guidelines for various offenses:

Offense First Offense Second Offense Third Offense
Minor Misconduct Warning 1 Day Ban 3 Day Ban
Major Misconduct 3 Day Ban 1 Week Ban Permanent Ban
Exploiting 1 Week Ban Permanent Ban N/A

Roles

Here are the roles available in our RP server:

  • Police Officer: Enforces the law and maintains order.
  • Firefighter: Responds to fire emergencies and rescues.
  • Paramedic: Provides medical assistance and transportation.
  • Civilian: Engages in various activities and roles within the community.
  • Criminal: Engages in illegal activities, but must follow RP rules.

Contact

If you need assistance or want to report an issue, please contact one of our moderators or admins through the Discord server or in-game messaging.
